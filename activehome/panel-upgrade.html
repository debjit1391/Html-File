Electrical Panel Upgrades & Replacement

Your panel is your home’s electrical “control center.” If it’s outdated, undersized, or showing warning signs, upgrading isn’t just convenience—it’s risk reduction.

Schedule Service Get a Quote

Signs You May Need a Panel Upgrade

  • Frequent breaker trips or buzzing
  • Panel feels warm or has a burnt odor
  • You’re adding an EV charger, heat pump, or renovation
  • Lights dim when appliances start
  • Rust, moisture damage, or poor labeling
  • Limited space for new breakers

What You Get

  • Load evaluation and capacity planning
  • Breaker organization and clear labeling
  • Safer protection for modern electrical demand
  • Code-compliant installation and testing
  • Permit/inspection coordination when required
