When You Should Call

Don’t Wait If You Notice Any of These

  • Burning smell, buzzing, or heat at outlets/panel
  • Flickering lights or frequent breaker trips
  • Warm outlets/switches, sparking, or discoloration
  • New high-load additions (EV, heat pump, renovation)
  • Old panel, outdated wiring, or missing GFCI/AFCI protection
