About us

Founded by Meghna Chawla in 2015, Foster & Forge Foundation is a non-profit, Section 8 company seeking to transform the lives of millions of school children by bolstering their education with the relevant life skills to succeed in school and beyond. We rely on donations from supporters like you to make our work possible. If you are a corporate seeking social responsibility we welcome your partnership. Join the movement
